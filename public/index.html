About Our Rochester Hills Scrubbers Self-Serve
Dog Wash & Grooming Location


Our Scrubbers Self-Serve Dog Wash & Grooming location in Rochester Hills is a state-of-the-art professional grooming facility designed for every pet owner. We only perform services that a pet can emotionally and physically endure. We will not perform services that cause a pet undue stress. If the pet is stressed, we will stop work and discuss the options with the pet owner.

Our Rochester Hills store, which opened in November 2017, is the newest of our three Scrubbers locations. Located on the West side of Rochester Road, 1/4 mile North of M-59, our store is in the strip center next to CJ Mahoney's.

Our Rochester Hills grooming facility is the best choice for your dog and cat grooming needs. We are professionally trained groomers with 50-plus years of experience in handling dogs and cats throughout Metro Detroit.

Our top-rated Rochester Hills pet experts offer a wide variety of grooming services tailored to meet your pets grooming needs. Every service offered is with your pet in mind, and all grooming is done in a way that will elicit comfort and happiness.

Rochester Hills is a city in northeast Oakland County, in the northern outskirts of Metropolitan Detroit area. As of the 2010 census, the city had a total population of 70,995.
